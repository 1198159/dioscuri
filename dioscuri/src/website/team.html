About the project team

In January 2006 the Dioscuri project team started the development on the emulator for digital preservation. The development team consists of the following people:
  • Bram Lohman (Tessella Support Services plc.), main developer of components CPU, Video, BIOS, PIC, Keyboard.
  • Jeffrey van der Hoeven (National Library of the Netherlands), main developer of components FDC, PIT, BIOS, Clock, Mouse and GUI.
  • Chris Rose (Tessella Support Services plc.), main developer of the ATA controller and XML configuration functionality.
  • Bart Kiers (National Library of the Netherlands).

Bram, Bart and Jeffrey are the active developers on the project. You can contact them by mailing to:

blohman AT users.sourceforge.net
jrvanderhoeven AT users.sourceforge.net
bkiers AT users.sourceforge.net

The project is sponsored by the Koninklijke Bibliotheek, National Library of the Netherlands, the Nationaal Archief of the Netherlands, Planets and KEEP.
